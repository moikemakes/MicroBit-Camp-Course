💻 Step 1: Meet the Microbit

🌟 What's New?

Before we start coding, we need to meet our new digital friend! The Microbit is a tiny computer that will become the body of your pet. It has special parts that let us interact with it.

🛠️ What to Do

Take a look at your Microbit and find these special parts: * Buttons (A and B): We can use these to feed our pet or play with it! * LED Screen: The 25 little lights where we will draw our pet's face. * Sound: Our pet will be able to make cute noises through the speaker! * Touch Logo: The gold logo on the front can feel when you touch it!
