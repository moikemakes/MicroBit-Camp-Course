# 🚀 Step 5: Smart Pet Upgrade

## 🌟 What's New?
Now let's make our pet even smarter with sounds and movement sensors! The Microbit can feel when it is shaken, dropped, or tilted.

## 🛠️ What to Do
* **Shake to Wake:** Pets love to sleep! Let's wake them up by shaking.
  * Go to the `Input` toolbox and grab the `on shake` block.
  * Add a `show icon` block with a Surprised Face!
* **Sounds:** Let's add a cute noise when they wake up!
  * Go to the `Music` toolbox and grab a `play sound` block. Put it inside your `on shake` block!
