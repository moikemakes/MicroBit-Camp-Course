# 🎨 Step 2: Make Your Pet Face

## 🌟 What's New?
Every pet needs a face! We are going to use the `show leds` block to turn on specific lights to draw your pet.

## 🛠️ What to Do
1. **Choose your pet:** Is it a dog, a cat, a bunny, or a space alien? Give it a fun name!
2. **Draw the face:** 
   * Go to your code toolbox and find the `Basic` category.
   * Grab the `show leds` block and drag it inside the `on start` block.
   * Click the little squares to draw your pet's face! The white squares will light up.
