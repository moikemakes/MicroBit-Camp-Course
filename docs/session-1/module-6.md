# 🍎 Step 6: Feed Your Pet Game

## 🌟 What's New?
Let's make a game where you feed your pet to get a high score! We will use a **Variable** to keep track of your points.

## 🛠️ What to Do
1. **Make a Score:** 
   * Go to the `Variables` toolbox and click `Make a Variable`. Name it `Score`.
2. **Set the Score to Zero:**
   * Grab the `set score to 0` block and put it in your `on start` block.
3. **Gain Points:**
   * Whenever you feed your pet (like pressing Button B), let's get a point!
   * Grab the `change score by 1` block and put it in your `on button B pressed` block.
4. **Show Your Score:**
   * Grab the `show number` block from the `Basic` toolbox.
   * Put your `Score` variable block inside it. Put this at the end of your feed button code!
