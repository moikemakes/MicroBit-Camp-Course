# 📳 Step 4: Shake to Wake

## 🌟 What's New?
If your pet is sleeping, you can gently shake it to wake it up! We will use the **Shake** sensor to trigger an animation and sound.

## 🛠️ What to Do
When the Micro:bit is shaken (`on shake` block):
* Open eyes 👀
* Play a jump animation (show a few different faces)
* Play a happy sound 🎵
