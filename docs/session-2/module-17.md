# 🏆 Step 10: Final Challenge & Showcase

## 🌟 Final Challenge: Save Your Hero Pet!
Can your pet survive for 60 seconds? Your project must include:
✅ 2 sensors
✅ 2 variables
✅ Animation
✅ Feeding system
✅ Win or lose condition

**Pick your difficulty:**
* 🟢 **Easy Mode:** Keep pet alive for 30 seconds.
* 🟡 **Normal Mode:** Keep pet alive for 60 seconds.
* 🔴 **Hard Mode:** Pet loses hunger faster (every 1 second!).

## 🌟 Showcase Time
Walk around and try your friends’ pets. See if you can keep theirs alive!
Give feedback and vote on:
* 🎨 Most Creative
* 🧠 Smartest Pet
* 😂 Funniest Pet
* 🏆 Best Survivor

## 🤔 Reflection
Answer these 3 questions:
1. Which sensor was your favourite to use?
2. What was hardest to code?
3. What would you add next time?
