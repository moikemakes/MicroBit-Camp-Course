# 🤚 Step 5: Touch to Feed

## 🌟 What's New?
We used buttons to feed our pet before, but now we can use the **Touch Logo**! It feels like giving your pet a little pat on the head.

## 🛠️ What to Do
Use the `on logo touched` block from the `Input` toolbox.

When the logo is touched:
* Show food 🍎 (using LEDs)
* Increase happiness ❤️
* Play a happy sound
