# 📊 Step 6: Add Pet Needs

## 🌟 What's New?
Real pets have needs! They get hungry, sad, and tired. We will create **Variables** to track your pet's life stats.

## 🛠️ What to Do
Go to the `Variables` toolbox and create three new variables:
1. `Hunger`
2. `Happiness`
3. `Energy`

In your `on start` block, set each of them to 5:
* Hunger = 5
* Happiness = 5
* Energy = 5
