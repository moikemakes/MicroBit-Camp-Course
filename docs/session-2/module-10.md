# 🌡️ Step 3: Temperature Mood Pet

## 🌟 What's New?
Just like us, pets get hot in the summer and cold in the winter. We will use the **Temperature** sensor to change our pet's mood based on the weather!

## 🛠️ What to Do
Use another `if / else if / else` block.

### Rules:
* **If** temperature is high (e.g., > 30) → show tired/hot face 🥵
* **Else if** temperature is low (e.g., < 20) → show cold/shivering face 🥶
* **Otherwise** → show normal face 😊
