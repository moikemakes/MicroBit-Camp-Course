# 🤖 Step 1: Meet the Sensors

## 🌟 What's New?
Great job building your Hero Pet! 🎉 Today, you will upgrade your pet into a **smart digital companion** that can react to the real world using Micro:bit sensors.

Your Micro:bit can sense the world around it just like we do! It can detect light, temperature, movement, and touch.

## 🛠️ What to Do
Let's find the sensors we will use today:
* 🌞 **Light Level:** The LED screen can also act as a light sensor!
* 🌡️ **Temperature:** The Micro:bit has a built-in thermometer.
* 📳 **Shake Movement:** The accelerometer detects when it is moved.
* 🤚 **Touch Logo:** The gold logo on the front is a touch sensor.
