🌟 Step 7: Showcase Time!

🌟 What's New?

Awesome work, Pet Creator! It’s time to show off your hard work to your friends and see what they have built.

🛠️ What to Do

Find a partner and trade Microbits. Play with their pet and let them play with yours.

  • Did they use different feelings?
  • What was their highest score in the feeding game?

🏆 Challenge / Reflection!

Before we finish, think about these 3 questions:

  1. What was the most fun part about making your pet?
  2. What block was the trickiest to use, and how did you figure it out?
  3. If you could add any crazy feature to your pet next time, what would it be?
