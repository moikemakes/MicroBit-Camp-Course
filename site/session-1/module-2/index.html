🎨 Step 2: Make Your Pet Face

🌟 What's New?

Every pet needs a face! We are going to use the show leds block to turn on specific lights to draw your pet.

🛠️ What to Do

  1. Choose your pet: Is it a dog, a cat, a bunny, or a space alien? Give it a fun name!
  2. Draw the face:
  3. Go to your code toolbox and find the Basic category.
  4. Grab the show leds block and drag it inside the on start block.
  5. Click the little squares to draw your pet's face! The white squares will light up.

🏆 Challenge!

  • Can you make your pet look like your favorite animal?
  • Try turning all the lights on and off to see what happens!
